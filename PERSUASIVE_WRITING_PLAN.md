## Princípios de Escrita Persuasiva Aplicados

1. **Social Proof** - Credibilidade através de números e provas sociais
2. **Escassez/Urgência** - Creates fear of missing out
3. **Especificidade** - Números precisos são mais críveis
4. **Aversão à Perda** - Visualizar custo de NÃO agir
5. **CTA Direto** - Action-oriented, low friction
6. **Storytelling** - Conexão emocional e memória
7. **Autoridade** - Estabelecer credibilidade
8. **Redução de Repetição** - Não saturar palavras-chave

## 6. REDUZIR REPETIÇÃO DE "STRUCTURE"

| Ocorrência | Substituir por |
|------------|----------------|
| `"It's a structure problem"` (3a vez) | `"It's a structural failure"` |
| `"structure problem"` no meio de frases | `"operating system problem"` |
| `"structure" em contextos genéricos | `"system", "architecture"` |

## 7. STORYTELLING NAS FALHAS

### Adicionar em cada failure card:

```html
<div class="failure-story">
    <p>We saw this at a Series B company in fintech.
    The VP of Engineering spent 60% of his time arbitrating
    prioritization disputes that should have been
    resolved by a framework, not a person.</p>
</div>
```

### Texto sugerido para adicionar após cada failure:

| Falha | Frase de Story |
|-------|----------------|
| 01 - Strategic Direction | `We worked with a $40M ARR fintech. Their roadmap had 140 items. Not one had a business outcome attached.` |
| 02 - Disconnected Execution | `At a healthtech Series B, engineering shipped 23 features in Q3. Marketing learned about 19 of them after launch.` |
| 03 - Reactive Operations | `A Series A SaaS CEO told us: "My calendar is my strategy." She was working 70-hour weeks and losing her best engineers.` |
| 04 - Tools Without Method | `The average company we diagnose has 7 tools nobody can agree on. The argument is always the same: "my data is in X, not Y."` |
| 05 - Talent Without Structure | `Three senior engineers left in six months. Exit interviews said the same thing: "I had no authority to say no."` |
